Hello there 👋

I'm Julie, a photographer, a birth & postpartum Doula, and a mom of two kids aged 10 and 12, both born in Berlin.

I was then a foreigner, without any family around, and I would have loved to know that such support as doulas or postpartum doulas existed!

I am passionate about feminist questions such as birth, sexual health, reproductive justice and human rights.

I'm thrilled to be able to help people reappropriate their bodies, know their options and rights in the process of reproduction and birth, and support them as if i was family, in the vulnerable and often exhausting time of postpartum.

Qualifications 🏅

About my doula experience

  • I've been working as a birth and post-partum doula as well as Mütterpflegerin (Haushaltshilfe, registered with Krankenkasse) since the beginning of 2021
  • Certified prenatal, birth & postpartum doula (Center For Doula Pathways, 2020)
  • 2 days Spinning Babies Workshop (2023)

About my photography experience

  • I've been a free-lance photographer for more than 20 years in fields as diverse as fashion, product, architecture, event & portrait.
  • Now I concentrate more in fields linked to doula and birth work so everything related to family & babies, including birth :)

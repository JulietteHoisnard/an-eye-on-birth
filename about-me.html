My Story

I'm Julie Meresse — a certified doula and photographer based in Switzerland, working in French, Italian, English, and German.

My journey into birth work began with a deep fascination for the transformative power of childbirth. I believe every birth is unique and deserves to be honored — both as a lived experience and as a memory to be preserved.

As a doula, I provide continuous emotional and physical support throughout pregnancy, labor, and the postpartum period. As a photographer, I capture the raw beauty, the tenderness, and the joy of these extraordinary moments — images you will treasure for a lifetime.

I am passionate about empowering families and helping every parent feel informed, confident, and supported on their path to welcoming a new life.
